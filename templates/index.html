Interventions for Schools

Underperforming School Program

Identify the top 5 schools with the highest dropout rates. Allocate additional resources, teacher training, and mentorship programs specifically for them.

Best Practice Sharing

Analyze schools with the lowest dropout rates to identify successful strategies. Organize workshops for other school administrators to share these findings.

Interventions by Area

Rural Infrastructure Investment

If Rural areas show high dropout rates, analyze distance-to-school data. Propose new transportation routes or after-school programs to reduce travel burdens.

Urban Community Outreach

In Urban areas, dropout reasons might be different. Partner with local community centers to create safe study spaces and mentorship opportunities outside of school.

Interventions by Gender

Female Mentorship Programs

If dropout rates are higher for girls, launch mentorship programs connecting students with successful women in various professions to provide role models and support.

Skill-Based Workshops for Boys

If boys show higher dropout rates due to 'Lack of Interest', introduce vocational or tech-focused workshops to provide alternative, engaging learning paths.

Interventions by Caste

Targeted Scholarship Programs

Analyze if specific caste groups (e.g., SC/ST) have higher dropout rates linked to low income. Propose dedicated scholarship and financial aid programs to alleviate this burden.

Interventions by Age/Standard

Transition Year Support

Identify if dropout rates spike at key transition years (e.g., moving from primary to secondary). Implement special counseling and academic support programs for these specific standards.
